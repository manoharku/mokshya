WEDDING AND EVENTS

From magnificent galas to social soirees and meticulous meetings, Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort is an ideal venue to host your gatherings. With spacious venues and Breathtaking views, we ensure that your event turns out to be one-of-a-kind complemented by our team’s professionalism and assistance. Pick a venue according to your requirements and we’ll make sure it’s nothing less than a flawless get-together.

THE MANSION HALL

Large and spacious, The Mansion at Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, Parwanoo near Chandigarh, is the ideal venue for big events. The hall opens up to the balcony bar and the terrace giving our guests the mesmerizing view of the valley. Events held at night can experience the whole valley glittering beneath them as if celebrating the event in unison. The Mansion has a fully electronic dance floor which instantly livens up any event.
