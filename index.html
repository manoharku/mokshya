ABOUT MOKSHA

The Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort brings this experience exclusively to you. Elevated 5000 ft. above sea level, this Ayurvedic health spa resort serves as a tranquil paradise on earth surrounded by the magical and mystical Shivalik ranges. This best luxury resort in Himachal Pradesh is the ideal place for weekend getaways from cities like Delhi, Chandigarh, Shimla and Kasauli. At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, we promise you an experience beyond the ordinary. No matter where you are at the resort, it offers you the most spectacular view of the valley around. Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort is the ideal place where you can let go completely and allow the aura of the resort to infuse you with bliss and positive thoughts.
