OUR DINING

With a multi-cuisine restaurant and bar, we invite guests to indulge in an exceptional dining experience at one of the best hotels in Parwanoo, Himachal Pradesh. The gastronomical delights prepared by our culinary experts cater to the taste and preference of any food connoisseur in the city.

MOKSHA RESTAURANT

Moksha Restaurant delivers a truly unique dining experience for its guests. With cuisines ranging from Thai and Oriental to Continental and Indian, the restaurant brings the 'in' in international. The magic of the restaurant lies in the fact that the chef uses fresh produce which is locally sourced or grown within the property to cook the dishes. The cuisine relies heavily on the ingredients available in the region. Stone fruits such as peaches are integral part of the dishes here.

Moksha Restaurant takes the ordinary ingredients that we see or use daily and turns them into extraordinary dishes. The dishes at the restaurant are innovative and pleasantly surprise you every time. The restaurant extends to the terrace area to provide a great outdoor seating area with a spectacular view that elevates your dining experience.

MOKSHA BAR & LOUNGE

The Moksha Bar & Lounge serves refreshing liquor, cocktails and mocktails concocted by our bartender to satiate your thirst. The bar offers a unique space to relax and unwind after a busy day at the spa.
